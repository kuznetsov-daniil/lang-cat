The language school LinguaLion was founded in Moscow, Russia in 1990. The School is only two minutes walk from Central underground station, in North Moscow. Located on a main street, students have an easy access to shops, restaurants and cafés. The school is also a Top-Rated ISI (The Independent Schools Inspectorate) school achieving the maximum grading possible in all inspections (academic, welfare and management)

Reasons to choose the school

Greate location

The central location as well as the great connection to public transport allow a trouble-free arrival at your Schick Hotel.

Great hospitality

Accorded on conducted surveys the students appreciate the atmosphere at the school

Modern school

Modern school environment help to promote student-centric learning, engagement, collaborative learning.

Best leisure activities

Leisure activities provide people an opportunity to meet others students who share the same interests and sometimes even make friends.

Policies

Days Off

Days without class in 2019 (including national and regional bank holidays): 01.Jan.2019, 25.Dec.2019, 26.Dec.2019. School policy regarding bank holidays: full number of lost classes are made up.

Administrative Fee

School's administrative fee: 500 PLN.

The majority of language schools charge a fixed-rate registration fee for all courses, regardless of type or duration. This registration or enrollment fee is not an extra fee for using our booking service. It is a fee charged by the school and every student who attends the school has to pay it Whether you register through us or directly at the school, you will have to pay this fee. You can find the fee listed in the school's brochure or website. It is usually referred to as: registration fee, enrollment fee or administration fee. When comparing prices make sure that all these costs from the school are included.

Cancellation

Free cancellation for 48 hours. After that, cancel up to 5 days before course start and get a full refund, minus the service fee.
